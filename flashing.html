Flashing Steps

Important Notice

BEFORE FLASHING ROM LOGOUT ALL ACCOUNT FROM YOUR DEVICE

Flashing an Encrypted Device

  1. Boot into recovery.
  2. Wipe dalvik-cache, data, and metadata.
  3. Flash ROM.
  4. Reboot recovery.
  5. Format data.
  6. Reboot system.

Flashing from a Decrypted ROM

  1. Boot into recovery.
  2. Reboot recovery.
  3. Wipe dalvik-cache, data, and metadata.
  4. Reboot system.

Dirty Flashing a Decrypted ROM

Note: Not recommended. Only proceed if the developer confirms dirty flashing is available.

  1. Reboot into recovery.
  2. Flash ROM.
  3. Reboot recovery.
  4. Wipe dalvik-cache.
  5. Reboot system.

Support

Flashing carries risks. Ensure you have a backup and follow each step carefully.
