About NextOS

NextOS is a custom unofficial **Xiaomi HyperOS** based project. It stands unique from the rest by offering a perfect blend of **lots of customization**, a **smooth UI experience**, and **exceptional stability**. We are committed to providing an OS that feels native, performs flawlessly, and lets you personalize every aspect.

Our focus is on delivering daily-driver stability while integrating innovative features and maintaining performance, making it the ideal choice for Xiaomi users seeking more control and refinement.

Customization
